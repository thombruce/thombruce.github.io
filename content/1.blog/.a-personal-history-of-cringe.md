God I hate the word _'cringe'_. But I don't mind embracing that which I hate if it gets my point across. I have written a lot of _cringe_ content in the past. Nothing overtly bad, just... _lemme talk about personal branding and entrepreneurship and..._ y'know, that sort of thing. That sort of _I'm a wannabe entrepreneur_ douchebaggery. Which isn't to say that personal branding isn't something I still consider, or that I have given up on trying to succeed as an independent business owner; just that god I hate that vibe and it does not suit me.

I'm pretty sure it's all purged from the internet, but it can no doubt be found on a wayback machine somewhere. Me, writing about what I thought it was best I write about when I was trying to launch a business - or was I? Thinking back, I don't think I was trying to launch a business then.
